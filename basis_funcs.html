Basis FunctionsÂ¶

base Various basis function objects specialised for parameter learning.
base.Basis() The base Basis class.
base.LinearBasis([onescol]) Linear basis class, basically this just prepending a columns of ones onto X.
base.PolynomialBasis(order[, include_bias]) Polynomial basis class, this essentially creates the concatenation, Phi = [X^0, X^1, ..., X^p] where p is specified in the constructor.
base.RadialBasis(centres[, lenscale_bounds, ...]) Radial basis class.
base.RandomRBF(nbases, Xdim[, ...]) Random RBF Basis, otherwise known as Random Kitchen Sinks.
base.RandomRBF_ARD(nbases, Xdim[, ...]) Random RBF Basis, otherwise known as Random Kitchen Sinks, with automatic relevance determination (ARD).
base.FastFood(nbases, Xdim[, ...]) Fast Food basis function, which is an approximation of the random radial basis function for a large number of bases.
base.SigmoidalBasis(centres[, ...]) Sigmoidal Basis
